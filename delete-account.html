Account and Data Deletion

2026 Capsule uses Google Sign-In only to identify the user on the device.

Data Storage

All recordings and app data are stored locally on your device. 2026 Capsule does not operate any external servers and does not upload or store user data remotely.

How to delete your data

  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data from your device.

Account deletion request

If you have signed in using Google and would like your account association removed, you may request deletion by emailing:

nobrainerhq@gmail.com

Please include the Google account email used to sign in. Requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe.

Data retention

No user data is retained on any servers. Once the app is uninstalled, all data is permanently deleted.
