Account and Data Deletion – 2026 Capsule

This page explains how users of 2026 Capsule can request deletion of their account and any associated data, in accordance with Google Play policies.

How to request account deletion

2026 Capsule uses Google Sign-In (OAuth) for authentication. The app does not store user accounts or personal data on external servers.

You can request account deletion in one of the following ways:

  1. Uninstall the 2026 Capsule app from your device.
  2. If you want explicit confirmation, send an email from the Google account used to sign in to:

    nobrainerhq@gmail.com
  3. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – 2026 Capsule

What data is deleted

  • All recorded videos, messages, and app data stored locally on the device are deleted immediately when the app is uninstalled.
  • No personal data is stored on external servers.
  • No backups are retained after deletion.

Data retention

2026 Capsule does not retain user data after app deletion. There is no additional retention period.

If an email request is sent, confirmation is provided once the request is reviewed.
